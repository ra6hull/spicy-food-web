Reference — Delivery Process & Quality Standards

This page describes how Spicy Food handles orders, ensures food safety and packaging standards, partners with kitchens, and what you can expect as a customer or partner.

Home

Delivery Process — Step by step

  1. Order placement: Customer selects dishes, confirms address, and pays (or chooses COD if available).
  2. Order acceptance: Restaurant/partner receives the order and confirms preparation time in the partner panel.
  3. Preparation: Food is prepared under monitored conditions. Estimated preparation time is updated to the customer.
  4. Pickup: A delivery rider assigned to the order picks up the sealed package and scans it (order ID) to mark pickup time.
  5. In-transit: Rider follows the most efficient route. Customers receive ETA updates via SMS/push (if enabled).
  6. Delivery & Confirmation: Rider delivers order; customer confirms receipt. Contactless drop-off option is available.
  7. Feedback & Rating: Customer can rate the meal and delivery; partner receives performance metrics.

Key SLAs

  • Order acceptance: within 5—10 minutes.
  • Pickup after ready: within 10—15 minutes.
  • Delivery time target: 25—45 minutes depending on distance and traffic.

Food Safety & Hygiene

We follow strict hygiene protocols to ensure every meal is safe and tasty.

  • Kitchen checks: All partner kitchens must pass an initial onboarding checklist: clean surfaces, separate raw/cooked food areas, handwashing facilities, and proper storage temperatures.
  • Temperature control: Hot items maintained >60°C until packaging; cold items kept below 8°C until dispatched where applicable.
  • Personal hygiene: Gloves, hairnets, and frequent handwashing are required for prep staff. No bare-hand contact with ready-to-eat foods.
  • Expiry & labeling: Ingredients and pre-made items must be labeled with manufacturing/use-by dates and stored correctly.
  • Periodic audits: Random spot-check audits and hygiene retraining for partners.

Packaging Standards

Packaging protects food quality, temperature, and prevents leakage while minimizing waste.

  • Sealed containers: All hot items are served in leak-resistant, insulated containers with tamper-evident seals.
  • Separation: Sauces and gravies are packed separately to avoid sogginess (where applicable).
  • Sustainable options: We encourage biodegradable or recyclable packaging for partners; plastic use is minimized.
  • Cutlery & condiments: Optional and included only on request to reduce single-use waste.

Ordering, Payment & Refund Policy

Payments

We support online payments via major Indian gateways (cards, UPI, wallets) and Cash-on-Delivery where enabled.

Order confirmation & cancellation

  • Customers can cancel before the restaurant accepts the order for a full refund (where payment was taken).
  • If the restaurant has already started preparing the meal, cancellation may be partial/refund at our discretion.

Refunds

Refunds for issues (wrong item, missing item, cold/spoiled food) can be requested via Contact or the order page within 24 hours. We investigate and process refunds within 3–7 business days.

Partner (Kitchen) Onboarding

We partner with small restaurants and cloud kitchens that meet our standards.

  1. Register using the partner form and provide FSSAI (or local) license and business details.
  2. On-site or virtual inspection by our operations team.
  3. Menu & pricing review: we advise on portioning and pricing for consistency.
  4. Training: Hygiene, packaging standards, and partner panel usage.
  5. Go-live: monitored launch with support for the first 7–14 days.

Sustainability & Community

We aim to reduce environmental impact and support local communities.

  • Packaging reduction: optional cutlery and biodegradable packaging for customers who opt-in.
  • Food waste partnerships: surplus food donation programs with local NGOs (subject to safety protocols).
  • Local sourcing: preference to local producers to reduce transport footprint and boost local economy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I request a refund?

A: Use the Contact form with order ID and photos (if relevant). We respond within 24–72 hours.

Q: Can I schedule an order for later?

A: Yes — we support scheduled orders via the order page when the restaurant enables it.

Q: Do you deliver to remote areas?

A: Delivery coverage depends on partner kitchens and rider availability. Enter your address at checkout to see availability.

Q: Are allergens listed?

A: Menu items include short descriptions. For allergen concerns (nuts, gluten, dairy), contact us before placing the order; partners must disclose common allergens.
